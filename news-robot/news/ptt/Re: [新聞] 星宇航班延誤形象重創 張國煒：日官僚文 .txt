成田機場起降規定如下:
No take-off or landings shall be permiited during the hours 1500-2100Z with
exception of aircraft in emergency or unavoidable situation.

意思就是世界時1500Z(日本當地UTC+9 即午夜24:00)開始有宵禁 禁止任何起飛落地
除非你是emergency 或是 unavoidable sutuation才會讓你飛
