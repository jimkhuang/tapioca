Chipmaker Arm to make its own semiconductor - FT

原文內容：

(Reuters) - British chipmaker Arm Ltd is building its own semiconductor to
showcase the capabilities of its products, as it seeks to attract new
customers and fuel growth following its Initial Public Offering (IPO) later
this year, the Financial Times reported on Sunday.

（路透社） - 英國晶片製造商Arm Ltd正在建造自己的半導體，以展示其產品的功能，因
為它尋求在今年晚些時候首次公開募股（IPO）後吸引新客戶並推動增長，英國《金融時
報》周日報導。

Arm will team up with manufacturing partners to develop the new
semiconductor, FT said, citing people briefed on the move, adding that the
company has built a new "solutions engineering" team that will lead the
development of these prototype chips for mobile devices, laptops and other
electronics.

英國《金融時報》表示，Arm將與製造合作夥伴合作開發新的半導體，並援引知情人士的
話說，該公司已經建立了一個新的“解決方案工程”團隊，將領導這些原型晶片的開發用
於行動裝置，筆記型電腦和其他電子產品。

The SoftBank Group Corp-backed company's newest chip, on which it started
work in the past six months, is "more advanced" than ever before, FT said,
citing industry executives.

英國《金融時報》援引行業高管的話說，軟銀集團支援的最新晶片在過去六個月中開始工
作，比以往任何時候都“更先進”。

The chip designer has no plans to sell or license the product and is only
working on a prototype, FT said.

英國《金融時報》表示，晶片設計師沒有計劃銷售或許可該產品，只是在開發原型。

Arm is a major supplier of intellectual property to many chip companies,
especially in mobile phones and has partnerships with major chip contract
manufacturers.

Arm是許多晶片公司的主要知識產權供應商，特別是在手機領域，並與主要的晶片合約製
造商建立了合作夥伴關係。

Earlier this month, Intel Corp (NASDAQ:INTC) said it will work with Arm to
ensure that mobile phone chips and other products that use Arm's technology
can be made in Intel's factories.

本月早些時候，英特爾公司（納斯達克股票代碼：INTC）表示將與Arm合作，確保使用Arm
技術的手機晶片和其他產品可以在英特爾的工廠生產。

Arm did not immediately respond to a Reuters request for comment.

Arm沒有立即回應路透社的置評請求。
