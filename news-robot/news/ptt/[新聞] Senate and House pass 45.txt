6 MIN AGO

Senate overwhelmingly votes to keep government funded for next 45 days
The 45-day continuing resolution passed in the Senate at 9:03 p.m. ET, less
than three hours before the federal government would have shut down.

The stopgap bill passed in a 88-9 vote.

— Kevin Breuninger
