原文內容：

TAIPEI, Nov 14 (Reuters) - Apple Inc (AAPL.O) supplier Foxconn (2317.TW)
reported on Tuesday a surprise 11% increase in third-quarter profit, boosted
by strong demand for smart consumer electronics ahead of the year-end holiday
shopping season in Western markets.

The Taiwanese company, the world's largest contract electronics maker, said
net profit for the July-September quarter rose to T$43.1 billion ($1.3
billion) from T$38.8 billion in the same period the previous year.

The profit beat a T$34.5 billion LSEG SmartEstimate, which gives greater
weight to forecasts from analysts who are more consistently accurate.

Foxconn said revenue for the fourth quarter would slightly decline year on
year, but did not give a reason and maintained its outlook for full-year
revenue to also slightly decline.

The world's biggest assembler of iPhones said it expects revenue for its
smart consumer electronics division, which includes smartphones, to also fall
slightly in the fourth quarter. The division makes up about half of Foxconn's
total revenue.

($1 = 32.3430 Taiwan dollars)

台北，11月14日（路透社）- 蘋果公司（AAPL.O）的供應商鴻海（2317.TW）周二公佈，
第三季淨利潤較去年同期增長11%，達到新台幣431億元（合13億美元），創下意料之外的
好成績，受惠於西方市場年終購物季對智慧型消費電子產品的強勁需求。

這家台灣公司是全球最大的代工電子製造商，表示今年7月至9月的淨利潤較去年同期的新
台幣388億元有所增加。

這一利潤超過了LSEG SmartEstimate的新台幣345億元的預測，該預測對於更一致準確的
分析師的預測給予了更高的權重。

鴻海表示，第四季的營收將輕微下降，但沒有給出原因，並維持了全年營收也將輕微下降
的展望。

這家全球最大的iPhone組裝商表示，其智慧型消費電子產品部門（包括智慧手機）的營收
在第四季也將輕微下降。該部門佔鴻海總營收的一半左右。

（1美元=32.3430新台幣）
