原文內容：
PARIS, Jan 2 (Reuters) - Unlisted French shipping group CMA CGM increased its

container shipping rates from Asia to the Mediterranean region

by up to 100% as of Jan. 15 compared to Jan. 1, it said on its website on Tuesday.

A CMA CGM spokesperson declined to provide further details on the rate hikes

when contacted by Reuters.

Shares in shipping companies have risen since attacks by Yemen-based Houthi militants

on some vessels in the Red Sea on expectations that longer routes will result

in higher freight rates.
